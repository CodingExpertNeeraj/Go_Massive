The MILES
Challenge

Amplus Solar announced the much-awaited
results of The MILES Challenge at EnergyScape
– Annual Conclave on Energy by Mint. Virya
Batteries, with their ultra-safe lithium-ion and
sodium-ion cell technology; SmartHelio (LED
Safari Innovation Pvt. Ltd.), with their edge
-based IoT sensor and intelligent software for
increasing the life of solar PV and batteries,
and Vecmocon Technologies Pvt. Ltd. with their
innovations around the core components of
electric vehicles, were announced as the
winners.
