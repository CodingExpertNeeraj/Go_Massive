Massive Earth
Summit​

Massive Earth Summit is India’s first grand
coalition where the private sector is joining
forces with policymakers & innovators to find
solutions for pollution & climate change. Last
hosted on June 06, 2018, the event saw 250+
delegates, 25+ institutional & corporate
partners & 3 partner countries.
